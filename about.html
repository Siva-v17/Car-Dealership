Our Story

BUILT BY CAR PEOPLE

Stackly Motors started as a single showroom in Salem in 2016. A decade later, we're a technology-led network connecting buyers with 42 verified dealerships across South India.

Our Mission

Make buying a car feel like
buying anything else — fast,
transparent, and on your terms

The traditional dealership model rewards friction: opaque pricing, pressure tactics, and paperwork that takes days. We rebuilt the process around the buyer — digital-first, dealer-verified, and backed by real people when you need them.

Timeline

A decade on the road

2016

First showroom opens in Salem

Founded by three ex-automotive engineers with a single lot and 40 vehicles on consignment.

2019

Certified pre-owned program launches

The 150-point inspection standard is introduced, becoming the backbone of buyer trust.

2022

Dealer network expands to 30+ partners

Stackly shifts from single-lot retailer to a marketplace connecting buyers with vetted dealers.

2024

Electric vehicle desk & home test drives

Dedicated EV specialists and doorstep test drives roll out across Tamil Nadu.

2026

1,200+ live listings, 42 dealers, one dashboard

Buyers and dealers now manage the entire journey — search, financing, paperwork — in one place.

2027

Pan-India expansion begins

Roadmap in motion: Stackly plans to onboard dealer networks in Karnataka, Kerala, and Andhra Pradesh next.
